About This Platform

Welcome to StepInAluFab, the complete digital fabrication suite for aluminium and glass works. This platform unifies all fabrication calculators, estimators, and measurement tools into one system designed to simplify, modernize, and professionalize project planning and execution.

From residential apartments to high-rise commercial façades, this digital suite supports all fabrication scales with zero guesswork and full transparency. The system automatically generates:

  • Exact cutting sizes (frame, leaf, glass, net)
  • Leftover & waste calculations
  • Profile consumption charts
  • Full glass dimension listings
  • Project costing & estimates
  • Cutting diagrams for workshops

Our Mission

To simplify aluminium and glass fabrication by providing a complete digital system that ensures accurate measurements, transparent cutting, and professional project planning across all levels of fabrication work.

Our Vision

To become the leading global fabrication support suite that empowers fabricators, builders, and clients with innovative digital tools that guarantee precision, prevent material loss, and transform the standards of aluminium and glass execution.

Our Story

StepInAlufab was founded in 2025 out of a practical and industry-rooted need: to make aluminium and glass fabrication accurate, efficient, and loss-free.

The platform’s origin began in the workshop, not in theory. Daily experiences with cutting errors, material shortages, supplier deductions, and manual calculation stress revealed a critical gap: Fabricators needed a reliable system that calculates everything before cutting begins.

What started as a simple attempt to solve personal fabrication challenges evolved into a full-service digital assistant supporting contractors, project managers, builders, workshop technicians, and learners throughout the entire fabrication journey.

About the Founder

Stephen Tekonembe Wagya is a committed aluminium and glass fabricator with hands-on technical experience gained through apprenticeship and active workshop practice. With more than three years of real field involvement, he has experienced both the strengths and struggles of fabrication at close range.

His apprenticeship under Dominic Aluminium & Glass Fabrication provided deep exposure to sliding and swing systems, shop front structures, net assemblies, office partitions, and commercial glass installations.

Stephen observed firsthand that manual calculations lead to waste, repeated costs, delays, and loss of trust. This inspired his mission to build a platform that helps every fabricator — from learner to master — work with confidence and precision.
